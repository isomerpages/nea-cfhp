<h4><strong>How do I identify an eligible Induction Stove in stores?</strong></h4>
<p>The following type of Induction Stoves are eligible for purchase with the Climate Vouchers:</p> 

<ul style="margin-top: 0.3em; margin-bottom: 0.5em;" data-tight="true" class="tight"> <li style="margin-bottom: 0.2em;">Built-in induction stove</li> 
	<li style="margin-bottom: 0.2em;">Portable induction stove</li> 
	<li style="margin-bottom: 0.2em;">All-in-one built-in induction stove and integrated hood</li> </ul>
	
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.3em;">Given that retailers may offer different models, it is advisable to enquire with them about the specific models that are eligible for purchase with the Climate Vouchers.</p> <br> 
	
<p style="margin-top: 0.3em;">You may visit <a href="http://www.go.gov.sg/cv-spend" class="Hyperlink SCXW28153244 BCX8" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="\_blank"><u>www.go.gov.sg/cv-spend</u></a> to locate your nearest participating retailer selling induction stoves.</p>
