<h4><strong>How do I identify an eligible Induction Stove in stores?</strong></h4>
<p>The following type of Induction Stoves are eligible for purchase with
the Climate Vouchers:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Built-in induction stove&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Portable induction stove&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All-in-one built-in induction stove and integrated hood&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Given that retailers may offer different models, it is advisable to enquire
with them about the specific models that are eligible for purchase with
the Climate Vouchers.</p>
<p>You may visit <a href="http://www.go.gov.sg/cv-spend" class="Hyperlink SCXW28153244 BCX8" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><u>www.go.gov.sg/cv-spend</u></a> to locate your
nearest participating retailer selling induction stoves.</p>
