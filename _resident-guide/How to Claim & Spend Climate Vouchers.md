<h3>How to spend your Climate Vouchers?</h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">



	
<li>
		<p><strong>Present proof of address with voucher</strong>.</p></li>
<li>
	<p><strong>Choose the amount to spend</strong>. Then tap on ‘Show voucher’.</p>
