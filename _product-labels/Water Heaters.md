<h4><strong>How do I identify an eligible Water Heater in stores?</strong></h4>
<p>The Mandatory Energy Labelling Scheme (MELS) was introduced for <a href="https://www.nea.gov.sg/our-services/climate-change-energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency/household-sector/regulated-goods" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">regulated goods</a>&nbsp;to
help consumers compare the energy efficiency and make more informed purchasing
decision</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Following the&nbsp;introduction of the&nbsp;MELS for water heaters from
1 Apr 2026, the eligibility criteria for water heaters will be revised
to 5-tick water heaters.&nbsp;</p>
